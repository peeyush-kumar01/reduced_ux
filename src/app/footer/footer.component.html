Drug Impurities Reference Standards
We offer a large selection of reference standards of drug-related process impurities, drug degradation products, including compendial impurities listed by USP, EP and other compendia. We strive to offer complete sets of drug impurities in line with analytical target profiles of drug substances. Many of our products are not available from any other manufacturer.
Drug Metabolites Reference Standards
Reference standards of drug metabolites are used in bioanalysis to positively identify and quantitate drug metabolites in the blood, urine and other body fluids. Our reference standards include products of oxidative metabolism and conjugation such as O-glucuronides, N-glucuronides, acyl glucuronides, and sulphates.
Drug Metabolites Stable Isotope Labeled Reference Standards
Reference standards of stable isotope-labeled drug metabolites are used in bioanalysis to quantitate drug metabolites in the blood, urine and other body fluids. We offer a large selection of deuterium (2H)-, 15N and 13C-labeled O-glucuronides, N-glucuronides, acyl glucuronides, and other drug metabolites with high chemical and isotopic purity.
Drug Substance Reference Standards
We offer a large selection of high purity reference standards of d rug substances. Our drug substance reference standards are traceable to the compendial reference standards.
Drug Substance Stable Isotope Labeled Reference Standards
We offer deuterium (2H)-, 15N, 13C- labeled drug substance reference standards with high isotopic and chemical purity. The isotopic purity is determined by mass spectroscopy. The labeling is designed to eliminate the possibility for isotope exchange and are tailored to the needs of the clients.
Fine Labeled Research Chemicals
This category includes non-drug related research chemicals labeled with stable isotopes. The isotopic purity is confirmed by high-resolution mass spectroscopy. The labeling is designed to eliminate the possibility of isotope exchange and is tailored to the needs of the clients.
